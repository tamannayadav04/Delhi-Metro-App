About Delhi Metro

Transforming urban transportation in the National Capital Region since 2002

Our Story

Delhi Metro is a world-class rapid transit system serving Delhi, the National Capital Territory of India, and the surrounding satellite cities. It is one of India's largest and busiest metro systems, ranking among the world's top metro networks in terms of length and number of stations.

The Delhi Metro has transformed the way people travel in the Delhi NCR region, providing a safe, reliable, and efficient transportation option for millions of commuters daily.

Our Mission

The mission of Delhi Metro Rail Corporation is to provide safe, reliable, comfortable, efficient, and economical transit services to the people of Delhi and the National Capital Region.

We aim to reduce traffic congestion, air pollution, and travel time, while promoting sustainable urban development through integrated planning and world-class infrastructure.

Our Journey Through the Years

The evolution of Delhi Metro from inception to becoming India's premier urban transit system

1
1995

DMRC Formation

Delhi Metro Rail Corporation was established as a joint venture between the Government of India and the Government of Delhi.

2
2002

First Line Inauguration

The first section of the Red Line from Shahdara to Tis Hazari was inaugurated, marking the beginning of metro operations in Delhi.

3
2010

Commonwealth Games Expansion

Major expansion before the Commonwealth Games with new lines and stations, significantly increasing the network coverage.

4
2018

Phase III Completion

Completion of Phase III with the addition of Pink and Magenta Lines, making Delhi Metro one of the largest metro networks in the world.

5
2020-Present

Expansion Continues

Ongoing work on Phase IV with extensions to existing lines and new corridors to connect more parts of the NCR region.
